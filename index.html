It is noted that the regional boundaries defined for this report do not align exactly with the application of the waste levy. The levy zone includes 39 local governments as shown in the published Levy Zone Map6. The levy generally applies to the seven eastern regions adopted in this report other than the Remote Queensland region, with some exceptions:

•Mount Isa City Council which is in the Remote Queensland region, is in the levy zone

•Balonne Shire Council which is in the Darling-Downs Maranoa region, is the only council in that region which is not in the levy zone

•Some of the smaller indigenous local government areas such as Yarrabah, Cherbourg, Woorabinda and Palm Island are excluded from the levy zone
